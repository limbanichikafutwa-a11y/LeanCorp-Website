DMAIC Improvement Projects

DEFINE

Identify cooperative challenges such as high post-harvest losses, machine downtime, and quality defects.

MEASURE

Collect baseline data on throughput, waste levels, cycle times, and machine efficiency.

ANALYZE

Root cause analysis using process mapping, fishbone diagrams, and Pareto analysis.

IMPROVE

Implement low-cost improvements, workflow redesign, equipment optimization, and operator training.

CONTROL

Standard operating procedures, visual controls, KPIs, and cooperative-led monitoring.

Typical Project Areas:

  • Groundnut shelling & oil extraction
  • Maize milling & storage
  • Post-harvest handling
  • Energy & fuel efficiency
